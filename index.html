The Future of Storytelling

Plutopia has quickly built a global audience of over 9 million followers and generated more than 5 billion views across platforms, creating viral content and original IP at scale.

Our work has partnered with leading brands including ASUS, Mountain Dew, Microsoft, and Clarks, blending storytelling, innovation, and culture to reach audiences worldwide.

Founded and led by Sage Shepard — a Webby Award–winning director and marketer, as well as a Billboard Music Award and MTV VMA recipient — Plutopia is built on the belief that the future of storytelling lies at the intersection of traditional craft and emerging technology.

With a deep respect for cinematic language, performance, and world-building, Plutopia merges proven filmmaking approaches with the power of AI — where humans remain the creative heart and mind, and AI becomes the hands that bring ideas to life at scale.

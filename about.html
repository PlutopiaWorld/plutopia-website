About the Founder

Sage Shepard, the founder of Plutopia, is an award-winning creator whose work spans animation, music, and digital storytelling. He has earned a Webby Award for animation, a Billboard Music Award as a singer and songwriter, and an MTV VMA, among other accolades. Constantly reinventing himself, Sage has always pushed the edge of what’s possible—now, with the rise of AI and next-generation creative tools, he sees this moment as the most exciting era yet for storytelling.
